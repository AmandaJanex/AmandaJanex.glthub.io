Homemade Pasta Sauce

Ingredients

  • 4.0 lbs Cherry Tomatos
  • 2 Fresno Peppers
  • 4 oz Parmesan Cheese (on rind)
  • 3 Sprigs Fresh Basil
  • 1 Small Acorn Squash

Instructions

  1. Get a pot big enought to hold all of your ingredients
  2. Add all of the ingredients to the pot
  3. Add water to Pot until about full
  4. Bring to a simmer
  5. Simmer for 48hrs stiring occationally
  6. Remove Cheese rind and other large skins
  7. Blend until smooth in a food Processor

For the first 24 hrs. you will simmer covered, and the second 24 hours will be uncovered, during the uncovered time be sure to not cook down too much, if needed add a small amounts of water to keep from reducing too far (burning). This is a simple yet delicious recipe that can be used in many Italian dishes. Enjoy!
