Homemade Pasta Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lbs Cherry Tomatos
  • 2 Fresno Peppers
  • 4 oz Parmasian Cheese (on rine)
  • Fresh Basil to taste
  • 1 Small Acorn Squash

Instructions

  1. Get a pot big enought to hold all of your ingredients
  2. Add all of the ingredients to the pot
  3. Add water to Pot until about full
  4. Bring to a simmer
  5. Simmer for 48hrs stiring occationally
